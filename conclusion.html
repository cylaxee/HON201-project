Significance and Conclusion

The original journal articles I looked at regarding this topic both had a heavy emphasis on the relationship between cooking and health observed in a certain group; I believe that both of these studies ignored the university student demographic. I think that there is value in exploring the relationship between the diets of specifically university students and their schedules. This research would hopefully encourage further research into this topic or a company to invest in healthier foods marketed toward university students with busy schedules; In addition, I hope that this research would encourage university students to open up more time in their schedules to dedicate towards a healthier diet.

Beyond the demographic of university students, this research would also highlight the relationship between having a busy schedule (in general) and the perceived healthiness of what an individual is eating. Further research into the topic of this relationship could be conducted on various different demographics ranging from low income families to single person households; again, companies could invest in some kind of new product or restaurant chain or something marketed towards these groups.
